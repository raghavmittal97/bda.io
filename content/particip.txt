We are pleased to invite you to the 8th International Conference on Big Data Analytics (BDA 2020). The conference will be held virtually during December 15-18, 2020, and will be organized by Ashoka University. BDA 2020 provides an international forum for researchers and industry practitioners to share their original research results, practical experiences and thoughts on big data from different perspectives including storage models, data access, computing paradigms, analytics, information sharing and privacy, redesigning mining algorithms, open issues and future research trends. 

The conference will have a strong technical agenda with

    5 keynote talks 

    5 invited talks 

    3 tutorials 

    1 panel 

    3 workshops (Political data science, Air Quality, Agricultural data science)

    14 peer-reviewed research papers
